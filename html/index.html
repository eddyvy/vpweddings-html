About us

VP Mallorca Weddings is focused on destination weddings, where our couples receive an attentive and personalized service.


Your wedding is not just a few hours of event, but a trip that must be perfect from beginning to end so that you can remember it as the most important and significant moments of your life.


Organizing a wedding is not an easy task, it takes time even if you are living on the island, but the process is even more complicated if you live abroad.


For this reason, we want to help you enjoy the path to your big day. No worries, no headaches.


We believe that the organization process is as important as the day of the wedding.


We want that process to be the greatest adventure of your life.
